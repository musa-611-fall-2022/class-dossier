My Experiences

Before the MUSA program, I studied at Colgate University, majoring in Mathematical Economics. The econometrics courses in this major, along with electives in GIS and data vizualization, entered me into this world of spatial data science, and I am excited to continue it at Penn.

After graduating in 2020, I’ve worked in the logistics industry as a delivery driver for Amazon and as a delivery cyclist for the Philly startup The Rounds. I enjoyed this work as I was interacting with spatial softwares and exploring the built environment, but knew it was temporary.

Since moving to Philly in fall 2021 I have been engaged civically, volunteering with the Fishtown Neighbors Association and advocating for better bicycle infrastructure and public transit with the politcal action committee 5th Square. Over the summer I interned in the City Controller’s office and hope to continue working for the City of Philadelphia once I graduate.
